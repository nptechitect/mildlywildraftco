Ohio River rafting with just enough thrill to keep you coffee in the cup.

At Mildly Wild Raft Co., we specialize in the perfectly average adventure. Think slow-moving currents, dramatic overreactions, and the occasional splash that might get your shoes wet. Whether you're an adrenaline junkie who forgot what Class V feels like, or just someone looking for a fun day on the water without losing your sunglasses—we’ve got the trip for you. So grab a paddle, slap on a helmet, and prepare to mildly panic your way down the majestic Ohio River. It’s kinda wet. Kinda wild. Kinda awesome.
